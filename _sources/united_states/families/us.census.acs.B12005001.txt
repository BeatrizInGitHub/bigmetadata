Population 15 Years and Over (5 subcolumns)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The number of people in a geographic area who are over the age of 15.  This is used mostly as a denominator of marital status.
