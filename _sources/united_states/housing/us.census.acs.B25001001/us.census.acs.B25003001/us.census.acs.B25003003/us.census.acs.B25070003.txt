Housing units spending 10 to 14.9% income on rent
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Gross rent from 10.0 to 14.9 percent of household income. Computed ratio of monthly gross rent to monthly household income (total household income divided by 12). The ratio is computed separately for each unit and is rounded to the nearest tenth. Units for which no rent is paid and units occupied by households that report no income or a net loss comprise the category, "Not computed". Gross rent as a percentage of household income provides information on the monthly housing cost expenses for renters.
