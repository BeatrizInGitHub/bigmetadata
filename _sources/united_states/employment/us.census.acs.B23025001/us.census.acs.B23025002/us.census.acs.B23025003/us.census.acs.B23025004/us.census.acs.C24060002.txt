Civilian Employed Population in Management, Business, Science, and Arts Occupations
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The number of employed civilians 16 years old and over in each geography in the Management, Business, Science, and Arts occupations. Occupation codes are based on the Standard Occupational Classification (SOC), published by the Executive Office of the President, Office of Management and Budget.
