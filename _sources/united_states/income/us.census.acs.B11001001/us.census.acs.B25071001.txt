Percent of Household Income Spent on Rent
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Within a geographic area, the median percentage of household income which was spent on gross rent.  Gross rent is the amount of the contract rent plus the estimated average monthly cost of utilities (electricity, gas, water, sewer etc.) and fuels (oil, coal, wood, etc.) if these are paid by the renter.  Household income is the sum of the income of all people 15 years and older living in the household.

      Obtain an estimate of "Percent of Household Income Spent on Rent" for a polygon
      using a combination of weighting by area and Households.

      .. code-block:: postgresql

          WITH data AS (
            SELECT 1 AS id,
            ST_Buffer(ST_SetSRID(ST_Point(-73.9, 40.7), 4326), 0.01) the_geom
          ),
          meta AS (
            SELECT OBS_GetMeta(ST_SetSRID(ST_Extent(the_geom), 4326),
            '[{"numer_id": "us.census.acs.B25071001",
               "denom_id": "us.census.acs.B11001001"}]'
            , 1, 1) meta
          FROM data)
          SELECT id, (data->0->>'value')::Numeric AS percent_income_spent_on_rent_estimate_2015_2015
          FROM OBS_GetData(
                 (SELECT ARRAY_AGG((the_geom, id)::geomval) FROM data),
                 (SELECT meta FROM meta))
