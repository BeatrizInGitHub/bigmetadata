Median after-tax income ($) (male)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------













.. tabs::



   
   .. tab:: {"tab_name": "Weighted by Income in 2010 of population aged 15 years and over not in economic families (male) "}

      Update a field of a table with an estimate of "Median after-tax income ($) (male)" for a polygon
      using a combination of weighting by area and Income in 2010 of population aged 15 years and over not in economic families (male).

      .. code-block:: postgresql

          WITH data AS (
            SELECT cartodb_id AS id, the_geom FROM <table_to_update>
          ),
          meta AS (
            SELECT OBS_GetMeta(ST_SetSRID(ST_Extent(the_geom), 4326),
            '[{"numer_id": "ca.statcan.cols_nhs.t013c082_m",
              "denom_id": "ca.statcan.cols_nhs.t013c079_m"}]'
            , 1, 1) meta
          FROM data)
          UPDATE <table_to_update> SET <field_to_update> = (data->0->>'value')::Numeric
          FROM OBS_GetData(
                (SELECT ARRAY_AGG((the_geom, id)::geomval) FROM data),
                (SELECT meta FROM meta)) obs_data
          WHERE <table_to_update>.cartodb_id = obs_data.id

      Obtain an estimate of "Median after-tax income ($) (male)" for a polygon
      using a combination of weighting by area and Income in 2010 of population aged 15 years and over not in economic families (male).

      .. code-block:: postgresql

          WITH data AS (
            SELECT 1 AS id,
            ST_Buffer(ST_SetSRID(ST_Point(-79.379, 43.655), 4326), 0.01) the_geom
          ),
          meta AS (
            SELECT OBS_GetMeta(ST_SetSRID(ST_Extent(the_geom), 4326),
            '[{"numer_id": "ca.statcan.cols_nhs.t013c082_m",
               "denom_id": "ca.statcan.cols_nhs.t013c079_m"}]'
            , 1, 1) meta
          FROM data)
          SELECT id, (data->0->>'value')::Numeric AS t013c082_m_estimate_2011
          FROM OBS_GetData(
                 (SELECT ARRAY_AGG((the_geom, id)::geomval) FROM data),
                 (SELECT meta FROM meta))

      NOTE: Although this measure is a median, the
      estimate will be a weighted average of medians.
