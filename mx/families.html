Families¶

Familial arrangements of people and households.

Columns¶

  • Birthrate
  • Birthrate to women age 15 to 49
  • Percentage of women age 15 to 19 with a child
  • Households (6 subcolumns)
    • Household with head between 30 and 59 years old (2 subcolumns)
    • Household with head 60 or more years old (2 subcolumns)
    • Female headed households (3 subcolumns)
    • Nuclear households (head with children under 18 years of age)
    • Male headed households (3 subcolumns)
    • Household with head under 30 years old (2 subcolumns)
  • Total population (5 subcolumns)
    • Population in households (6 subcolumns)
    • Population 15 to 24 years old (3 subcolumns)
    • Population 12 or more years old (5 subcolumns)
    • Female population (2 subcolumns)
    • Male population (2 subcolumns)
